 OUR STORY

The Burger Stand got its start in an Irish pub kitchen in 2009. since relocating to the Casbah at 803 Massachusetts in historic Downtown Lawrence and expanding to Topeka in 2011, The Burger Stand has a large menu of gourmet burgers, hawt doggz, a large vegetarian menu and so many sides (fries galore!) and a free sauce bar. Our bar menus specialize in classic, American cocktails as well as carefully curated craft beers on tap and over 100 bottles. Each location offers larger private rooms available for reservations.
